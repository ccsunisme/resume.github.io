Prof. Dr.-Ing. ChiChia Sun 宋啟嘉 特聘教授 工學博士

Prof. Dr.-Ing. Chi-Chia Sun received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from National Taiwan Ocean University (2000-2004) and Electronic Engineering from the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (2004-2006), respectively. From 2008 to 2011, he worked as a Research Assistant at TU Dortmund and received his Doktor Ingenieur degree. His research interests are in Digital IC Design, FPGA System Integration, Machine Learning, Image Processing and Verilog/FPGA design.
